Welcome to the Stowe Theater Guild

The Stowe Theatre Guild is a non-profit volunteer community theatre organization, pledged to present the best local theatre experience in New England and offering diverse, quality productions in a historic facility. For over fifty years, Stowe Theatre’s mission has included drawing from and giving back to our community.
